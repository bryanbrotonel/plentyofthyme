About Plenty of Thyme



Introduction

The Metro Vancouver area has a problem with food waste and that is mainly caused by the lack of awareness surrounding this issue. People are unaware of the impact food waste has on our environment and the amount of money they could be saving.

Why Use Plenty of Thyme?

Plenty of Thyme is a web based grocery application that will keep track of the items in your fridge as well as their expiry dates.
